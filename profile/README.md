**About NEDC**

The **Neural Engineering Data Consortium (NEDC)** is dedicated to advancing research in neural and biomedical engineering by focusing the scientific community on high-impact problems in signal and image processing.

Our mission is driven by three core efforts:

**Data Creation**

We develop and publish **large-scale, high-quality data corpora** specifically designed to support modern machine learning and data-driven research.

**Evaluation & Benchmarking**

We independently arbitrate algorithm testing and evaluation through structured **data challenges**, enabling fair and reproducible comparison of methods.

**Dissemination of Research**

We share results through peer-reviewed publications and our **IEEE-sponsored annual conference**, fostering collaboration and innovation across the field.

All NEDC datasets are **meticulously curated**, continuously maintained, and supported by a robust ecosystem of software tools. Our data is released **freely and without unnecessary restrictions** to maximize accessibility and impact within the research community.

**Datasets**

* **TUEG** – Temple University EEG Corpus 

* **TUAB** – Abnormal EEG Corpus

* **TUAR** – Artifact Corpus

* **TUEP** – EEG Epilepsy Corpus

* **TUEV** – EEG Events Corpus 

* **TUSZ** – Seizure Corpus

* **TUSL** – Slowing Corpus
